Some Features we do provide

Eleganc Modular System offers customizable office furniture with diverse design options, including ergonomic desks, workstations, and tables. Features include cable management systems, varied leg designs, durable materials like MS CRC, and customizable laminate finishes. The company emphasizes precision, durability, and innovative aesthetics, providing solutions for meeting rooms, training spaces, and collaborative environments with ergonomic and functional designs.

  • Customizable Designs

    Sizes and specifications can be tailored to client requirements for all products, including desks, tables, and workstations.

  • Material Options

    Laminate finishes in Merino, Action Tesa, or Greenlam.

    Aluminum powder-coated screens and dye-casted legs.

  • Leg Variations

    Options such as square, round, tapered, loop, and triangular legs with various dimensions (e.g., 40x40mm, 50x50mm)..

  • Modesty Panel Designs

    Perforated and laser-cut designs, including the option for custom branding (e.g., company logos).

  • Workstation Configurations

    Linear workstations, 4-way workstations, and corner workstations, supporting diverse workspace layouts.

  • Durability and Precision

    Use of MS CRC (cold-rolled steel) materials and advanced fabrication techniques for high durability.

  • Innovative Aesthetics

    Designer tables with laser-cut patterns and modern finishes.

  • Meeting Room Solutions

    Small and large meeting tables with integrated wire management and diverse leg design options (U-shaped, tapered, loop).

  • Ergonomic Focus

    Products designed to ensure comfort and functionality, meeting standard ergonomic guidelines.
